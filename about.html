About Us

Azccriminal or otherwise known as azencompiler open source foundation criminal is a community focused on software development from a different perspective. Our main goal is to provide a platform structure based on cybersecurity and technology. In general, you can access a large list of the software we have developed from the services section. To support us, you can visit our contact-us page to donate or support the source tree structure.

Our Mission

Our general mission is to present the open source structure in a more developed way and to undertake bigger projects compared to our previous foundation which went bankrupt. However, our community structure is not managed by a specific person or personality, the copyrights are managed by the founders of Azccriminal.

Our Core Values

  • Integration and development structure: the projects we develop are mostly focused on structure integration
  • Cybersecurity tree structure: we have web-based realtime projects developed for cybersecurity
  • Stabilization: We develop by giving great importance to stabilization, but for those who use stable structure, we make developments by providing unstable-based support in some projects.
